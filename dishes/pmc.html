Protein Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • oatmeal
  • Scoop of Protein Powder
  • Water

Directions

  1. Pour protein and oatmeal together. About a 1to1 ratio
  2. Add water until desired thickness is achieved. I enjooy it not being too runy.
  3. Put in the microwave for about 2 min or until desired consistancy.
  4. Enjoy!
