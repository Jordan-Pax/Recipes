Instapot Chicken

A real simple chicken recipe. Can make shreded chicken from this and put into eggs, burritos, tacos, or even have in by itself!

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock
  • Boneless, skinless, chicken breast(Thawed or frozen)
  • Italian seasoning, oregano, basil or thyme
  • Garlic powder
  • Brown sugar
  • Kosher salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. pour in some chicken stock.
  2. Season chicken breasts with all of the spices and herbs.
  3. Put the Chicken in the pot and lock the lid. Make sure vent is in the closed position.
  4. Pressure cook on high for 15 min
  5. Wait 10 minutes once it finishes, to allow it to naturually depressurize. Then CAREFULLY move the vent to open.
  6. Take out chiccken and enjoy! Make sure it's done though.
